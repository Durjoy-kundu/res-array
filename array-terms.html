Legal & Policy

Terms & Conditions

Please read our policies carefully before making a reservation or attending an event at Array Brasserie & Grill.

★

Service Charge Policy: A service charge of 10% will be applied to all bills. This charge is mandatory on Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays, and special occasions including Bank Holidays and New Year's Eve. A mandatory service charge also applies to all parties of 4 or more guests.

£

Deposits & Service Charge

  • A mandatory service charge applies to parties of 4 or more guests.
  • Deposits are deducted from the final bill on the day of your visit.
  • Deposits are fully refundable if the booking is cancelled at least 7 days in advance.
  • If cancelled 3–7 days prior, 50% of the deposit will be retained as a cancellation fee.
  • For cancellations within 72 hours, deposits are non-refundable but can be transferred to a rescheduled booking.
  • If the confirmed number of guests decreases, the deposit for any guests who do not attend will be forfeited.
✕

Cancellations & Guest Count

  • If guest numbers decrease, the deposit for any guest who does not attend will be forfeited.
  • Any increase in guest numbers must be confirmed in advance and is subject to availability.
  • Pricing is based on the number of guests confirmed at the time of reservation.
  • If the actual number of attendees is less than confirmed, no reduction will apply — the full amount for the confirmed booking will be charged.
  • If the number exceeds the confirmed count, additional guests will be charged at the same agreed per-person rate.
◷

Timing & Seating

  • Guests must adhere to their allocated arrival time and table duration of 3 hours.
  • Late arrivals may reduce the available dining time without adjustment to the booking end time.
  • Tables can only be held for 20 minutes past the booking time unless management has been notified.
  • Guests must vacate the venue by the official closing time of the restaurant.
🍽

Food & Beverage Policy

  • No outside food or drinks are permitted on the premises.
  • Birthday cakes are allowed but cannot be cut or served by staff unless a cake-cutting fee has been agreed.
  • Cakes brought for display or photo purposes will be stored at room temperature. Array Brasserie cannot accept liability for storage conditions.
⚠

Allergen & Dietary Information

  • Our dishes may contain or have come into contact with allergens including gluten, dairy, eggs, nuts, soy, fish and shellfish.
  • Please inform your server of any dietary requirements or allergies before ordering.
  • Whilst every care is taken, we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment due to shared kitchen facilities.
  • Guests with severe allergies are advised to consult with management before dining.
  • Menu descriptions do not always list all ingredients — our team is happy to provide full allergen information on request.
◈

Service & Billing

  • A maximum of two payments can be taken per table. Individual payments per person are not accepted.
  • Deposits are deducted from the final bill on the day of the visit.
  • Any additional services such as cake cutting, special setups, exclusive use, or staff-assisted decorations may incur extra charges.
◆

Special Events & Private Hire

  • All private hire bookings require a non-refundable deposit to secure the date.
  • Final guest numbers and set menu selections must be confirmed at least 5 business days prior to the event.
  • Any changes to the agreed setup, décor or catering requirements after confirmation may incur additional charges.
  • Array Brasserie reserves the right to adjust room layouts to meet fire safety and operational requirements.
  • Exclusive use bookings are subject to a minimum spend — details available upon enquiry.
✦

Decorations & Guest Behaviour

  • Only table-top decorations are permitted unless pre-approved by management.
  • Confetti, glitter, or materials that cause excessive cleaning are strictly prohibited. A cleaning fee may apply if used.
  • Guests must respect the venue, staff, and other diners. Disruptive or abusive behaviour will result in removal from the premises.
◈

Gift Card Terms

  • Array Brasserie gift cards are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.
  • Gift cards cannot be exchanged for cash and are non-refundable.
  • Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced.
  • Gift cards can be used across multiple visits until the balance is fully redeemed.
  • Gift cards cannot be used in conjunction with any other promotional offers unless stated otherwise.
  • Remaining balances below £1 will not be refunded.
⊕

Compliance & General

  • All guests must follow the venue's safety rules and respect operating hours.
  • Management reserves the right to modify seating arrangements to accommodate operational needs.
  • Array Brasserie reserves the right to refuse service or entry at its discretion.
  • These terms are subject to change without notice. The most current version will always be available on our website.
⊛

Privacy & GDPR

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Data We Collect

Name, contact details, booking information and dietary preferences provided during reservation or enquiry.

How We Use It

To manage your booking, communicate with you about your visit, and improve our service. We do not sell your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us to make a request.

Data Retention

Booking data is retained for up to 2 years for operational and legal purposes, then securely deleted.

For full details or to exercise your rights, contact us at info@arrayessex.com.
