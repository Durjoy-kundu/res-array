Where It All Began

A Family Vision,
Brought to Life

Array Brasserie & Grill opened its doors in 2020 — a year that tested the world, but could not dampen the spirit of a family determined to share their passion for food with Essex.

What began as a dream around a family table became a reality at 91 Shepherds Hill, Romford. From the very first day, our mission has remained unchanged: to create a space where guests feel genuinely welcomed, where every dish tells a story, and where every visit becomes a memory worth holding onto.

We are a family business in every sense of the word. The care that goes into our kitchen, our service and our spaces reflects the values we hold closest — hospitality, quality and a deep pride in everything we put our name to.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Our values aren't a statement on a wall — they're present in every dish we serve, every guest we welcome and every decision we make.

At Array, we believe that great dining is about far more than what's on the plate. It's the warmth of being greeted by name. It's the confidence of knowing exactly what you're eating and where it came from. It's the reassurance that every detail — from the sourcing of our ingredients to the training of our team — has been handled with care.

As a family business, our values run deep. We treat every guest the way we would want to be treated ourselves — with respect, attentiveness and a genuine desire to make their experience exceptional. We hold ourselves to high standards not because we have to, but because we wouldn't have it any other way.

These principles guide everything we do, from the way we design our menus to the way we run our events. They are the foundation of Array Brasserie & Grill — and they always will be.

Unwavering commitment to quality in every dish we serve
Genuine, attentive hospitality — every guest, every visit
Transparency about our ingredients and how they are prepared
Respect for our guests, our team and our community

Halal Certified

Fully Certified & Verified

Array Brasserie & Grill is proud to be fully Halal certified. Every item on our menu — from our grills to our sauces — is prepared in accordance with Halal standards, giving our guests complete confidence and peace of mind.

Our Halal certification is not an afterthought — it is a core part of who we are and a promise we take seriously.
