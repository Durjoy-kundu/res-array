Our Story

Crafted with Passion,
Served with Soul

Array Brasserie & Grill was born from a singular vision — to bring world-class Mediterranean flavours to the heart of Essex, paired with a dining atmosphere that feels both intimate and grand.

From sunrise brunches to sunset dinners, every dish celebrates carefully sourced ingredients and culinary artistry. We don’t just feed you — we create moments you carry with you.
